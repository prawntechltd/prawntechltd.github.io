1) Who we are (Data Controller)

For users in the United Kingdom, PrawnTech Ltd. is the “data controller” of your personal data for the purposes of the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

2) What the App does

DeScry is a spiritual diary that lets you record and reflect on “signs” you sense. You can optionally share entries with other DeScry users or via third-party apps such as WhatsApp (and other share destinations available on your device).

3) Information we collect

3.1 Information you provide

  • Diary content: text, notes, titles, tags, and any other content you type into the App (“Entries”).
  • Shared content: content you choose to share with other users or externally.
  • Support communications: information you include when you contact us (e.g., email address and message).

3.2 Information from your device and app use

We currently do not use advertising or third-party analytics SDKs in the App. We may still receive limited technical information that is necessary to operate the App and provide support, such as:

  • Basic device/app information you share with us when reporting a problem (e.g., iOS version, device model).
  • Crash or diagnostic information if you have enabled sharing of iOS diagnostics with Apple and it is made available to developers through Apple’s tools (where applicable).

3.3 Information we do not intentionally collect

  • We do not sell personal data.
  • We do not run in-app ads.
  • We do not use third-party analytics tracking within the App.
  • We do not intentionally collect precise location data.

4) How we use your information

We use information for the following purposes:

  • Provide the App’s features (e.g., storing and displaying your Entries; enabling sharing you request).
  • Operate and maintain the App (e.g., troubleshooting, performance, security).
  • Customer support (responding to your messages and resolving issues).
  • Legal and safety reasons (to comply with applicable law, enforce our terms, or protect rights and safety).

5) Legal bases for processing (UK GDPR)

We process personal data under one or more of the following legal bases:

  • Contract — where processing is necessary to provide the App to you.
  • Legitimate interests — to operate, improve, and secure the App and provide customer support (balanced against your rights).
  • Consent — where you choose to share content externally or enable optional device settings that provide diagnostics.
  • Legal obligation — where we must comply with the law.

The App allows you to share Entries with other DeScry users (if you choose) or externally via iOS sharing options (e.g., WhatsApp, Messages, email, social apps).
