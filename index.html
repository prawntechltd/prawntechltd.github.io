About Us

Prawn Tech is an education technology company founded in 2019 with a mission to enhance how people learn and retain knowledge. Combining outstanding tutoring with innovative digital tools, Prawn Tech develops apps and learning solutions grounded in science and built for real results. Behind the company is founder Eve Dale — a qualified chemistry teacher with a background in technology, medicine, and neuroscience — whose passion for effective learning drives everything Prawn Tech creates.
